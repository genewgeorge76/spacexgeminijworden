Our Story

Built on Four Generations of Family Craftsmanship

J. Worden & Sons Asphalt Paving was founded in 1984 with a single commitment: deliver paving that holds up for decades, not just seasons. What began as a local Chester, Virginia operation has grown into a multi-state commercial force — without ever sacrificing the family values that started it all.

Note: We are the original J. Worden family business and are entirely independent from any other Worden-named paving operations that may exist in Chester or the surrounding region. Our lineage, licenses, and client relationships are our own.

  • 1984

    J. Worden & Sons founded in Chester, Virginia. First-generation leadership sets the structural standards still used today.

  • 2000s

    Expansion into commercial markets. Multi-city Virginia contracts secured. Second and third generation enter the business.

  • 2015

    4th-generation leadership transition. New management modernizes operations while preserving the family's structural standards and reputation.

  • 2018–2021

    Four consecutive Best of Houzz awards earned, recognizing excellence in design and customer satisfaction.

  • Today

    Multi-state commercial paving partner for KFC, Arby's, and Taco Bell. Serving 41 cities across Virginia with our 6-inch compacted stone structural standard.

What We Do

Full-Spectrum Paving Services

Commercial Paving

Parking lots, drive-throughs, loading docks, and roadways for commercial properties. Full-depth construction with engineered sub-bases.

Residential Driveways

New driveway installation and full-replacement projects for homeowners who want commercial-grade durability underfoot.

Parking Lot Resurfacing

Mill-and-overlay resurfacing restores aging lots without the cost of full replacement. ADA-compliant striping included.

Sealcoating

Protective sealcoating that blocks UV damage, fuel spills, and freeze-thaw cycles — extending pavement life by years.

Crack Sealing

Hot-pour rubberized crack filling stops water infiltration before small fissures become expensive structural failures.

Grading & Drainage

Proper site grading and drainage design to prevent standing water — the number one killer of asphalt surfaces.

Structural Standard

The 6-Inch Compacted Stone Difference

Many paving contractors cut corners with minimal base preparation. We never do. Every J. Worden & Sons project begins with a 6-inch compacted stone structural base — the same engineering standard used in municipal roadways and high-traffic commercial sites.

This standard is applied across our 41-city Virginia service grid, from Richmond metro to the Shenandoah Valley, ensuring every client — whether a homeowner or a national chain — receives pavement built to last.
