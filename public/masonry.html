Our Services

Masonry & Hardscaping Services

Complementing our asphalt expertise with stone, brick, and concrete craftsmanship — all backed by the same uncompromising standards.

🧱

Retaining Walls

Engineered retaining walls in natural stone, segmental block, and poured concrete. Designed for both structural support and visual appeal, with proper drainage and compaction behind every wall.

🏗️

Concrete Flatwork

Sidewalks, patios, aprons, and curbing in standard, stamped, or exposed-aggregate concrete. Precision grading ensures proper drainage and a level, long-lasting surface.

🪨

Decorative Hardscaping

Custom stone patios, walkways, fire pit surrounds, and outdoor living spaces. We work with natural flagstone, travertine, bluestone, and premium pavers to create outdoor areas that last.

🔨

Masonry Restoration

Brick repointing, stone repair, chimney restoration, and structural masonry rehabilitation. We match existing materials and mortar profiles to preserve the character of historic and aging structures.

🏠

Steps & Entryways

Custom stone, brick, and concrete steps with handrails. Entryway upgrades that combine safety with curb appeal — built to code and designed to impress.

🌿

Landscape Integration

Hardscaping that works with your landscaping — retaining walls with planting pockets, permeable paver systems, and graded transitions between paved and planted areas.
