## WS-001: 96% Marshall Unit Weight Compaction Standard

**Standard:** Minimum 96% of Marshall Unit Weight on all asphalt lifts  
**AASHTO Reference:** AASHTO T245 — Marshall Mix Design  
**ASTM Reference:** ASTM D1559  
**Industry Floor:** 92% (Worden exceeds by 4 points minimum)  

### Specification
- All asphalt lifts must achieve ≥ 96% of the job mix formula (JMF) Marshall Unit Weight
- Measured by: Nuclear Density Gauge (ASTM D2950) or Coring (ASTM D5361)
- Frequency: Every 500 linear feet or every 1,000 sqft, whichever is more frequent
- Non-conforming areas: Remove and replace (no waivers)

### Rationale
Compaction at 96% vs. 92% extends pavement life from ~12 years to 20+ years. Reduces air voids that allow water infiltration and freeze-thaw cracking. Non-negotiable on all Worden projects.

## WS-002: 6-Inch VDOT-Grade Structural Stone Base

**Standard:** Minimum 6-inch compacted aggregate base on all paving projects  
**VDOT Reference:** Section 315 — Aggregate Base Course  
**Material:** No. 21A Crushed Stone (VDOT Specification)  

### Specification
- Minimum: 6 inches compacted depth (residential)
- Commercial/municipal: 8–12 inches based on traffic load (per VDOT pavement design)
- Compaction: 100% Standard Proctor per AASHTO T99
- Material: VDOT-approved No. 21A crushed stone only
- Subgrade: Must pass proofroll before base placement

### Rationale
An inadequate base is the #1 cause of premature asphalt failure. The Worden 6-inch minimum is non-negotiable and is the primary differentiator in quality.

## WS-003: $9/Ton Liquid Asphalt Oil Price Shield

**Standard:** All proposals include ±$9/ton liquid asphalt price buffer  
**Binder Reference:** PG 64-22 (AASHTO M320)  
**Base Price Reference:** Virginia DOT posted plant-mixed asphalt price  

### Specification
- All unit prices include $9/ton shield above and below the binder price on proposal date
- Shield activates when binder price changes by more than $9/ton from proposal date
- Activation triggers: Written change order to owner
- Buffer protects margin on projects lasting > 45 days from proposal

### Formula
`Adjusted Unit Price = Base Unit Price ± (Δ Binder Price / 110 tons per lane-mile × shield factor)`

## WS-004: Zero-Downtime Medical Compliance

**Standard:** All crew members maintain current DOT medical cards without operational gaps  
**Authority:** FMCSA 49 CFR Part 391  

### Specification
- DOT Medical Examiner Certificate reviewed at hire and 60 days before expiration
- Medical certification tracked in crew management system
- 30-day advance notice sent to crew member before expiration
- Replacement scheduling initiated if renewal pending during project window

## WS-005: QSR Fast-Track 90-Day Program

**Standard:** QSR/Fast Food new site construction from raw land to striped asphalt in 90 calendar days  
**Client History:** KBP/KFC, Arby's, Taco Bell, Winn-Dixie  

### Specification
- Day 1–14: Demolition, clearing, rough grading
- Day 15–30: Underground utilities, storm drainage
- Day 31–45: Subbase, aggregate base course placement
- Day 46–60: Asphalt paving (base course + surface course)
- Day 61–75: Concrete curb, gutter, sidewalks, ADA ramps
- Day 76–90: Final grading, striping, punch list, certificate of occupancy support
